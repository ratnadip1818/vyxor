About Vyxor

Built because I
couldn't afford the tools.

Vyxor exists for one reason — every existing trade journal cost more money than most traders make. So I built one for free. And then kept building.

Founder
Ratnadip
📍 Ahilyanagar, Maharashtra · Futures Trader

When I started trading futures, I knew journaling was important. Every successful trader said so. But every online journal wanted ₹2000–₹8000 per year — and as a trader still figuring things out, I didn't have that money to spare. So I built my own. That was Vyxor. Once it worked for me, I realized other Indian traders were probably stuck in the same place — knowing they needed these tools but priced out of them. So I made Vyxor free for everyone. Not freemium. Not a trial. Free.

The mission

Most retail traders fail not because they can't pick trades — they fail because they don't track them, don't size them right, and don't review their own behavior. The tools that fix all three exist. They just cost too much for the people who need them most.

Vyxor flips that. Position sizing, trade journaling, AI analysis, risk management — all the things institutional traders take for granted — built for retail traders who need them more, and offered free.
