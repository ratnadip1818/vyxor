What's inside

Built for traders who want
to understand themselves.

Every feature exists to help you learn from your own behavior — not to predict the market.

01
Trade Journal
Log in 30 seconds. Asset, direction, P&L, emotion, setup. Whether the trade was planned or impulsive. Whether you followed your rules. Your own data, organized.
02
Emotion Tracking
Tag every trade with how you felt before and after. Over time you see which emotional states influenced your decisions and which ones led to disciplined execution.
03
Journal AI
Reads your journal entries and surfaces behavioral patterns. Not trade advice — self-reflection. Which sessions are best. Where you break your own rules. What it costs you.
04
Weekly Summary
Every Sunday at 9am — a summary of your trading week in your inbox. P&L, win rate, top emotion, discipline score. Sent at your local time.
