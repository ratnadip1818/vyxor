What is Vyxor

Every time you log a trade, you capture not just the result — but the emotion you felt, the setup you followed, whether the decision was planned or impulsive. Over time, the AI reads all of it and shows you the exact patterns that are costing you money. Not generic advice. Your data. Your behavior. Your blind spots.
