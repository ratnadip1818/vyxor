FAQ
Common questions
Is Vyxor really free to start?
+
Yes. You get a full 15-day free trial with every feature unlocked — unlimited trades, AI analysis, weekly reports, CSV import, everything. No credit card required. After 15 days, Pro is ₹199/month — less than a single bad trade costs most traders.
Can I import trades from Zerodha, Groww or Angel One?
+
Yes. Download your tradebook CSV from your broker console and upload it directly to Vyxor. Buy/sell legs are automatically matched into complete round-trip trades. P&L is calculated using correct Indian lot sizes — NIFTY = 25 units, BANKNIFTY = 15 units, and so on.
Does Vyxor need access to my broker account?
+
No. Vyxor never connects to your broker. You download a CSV from your broker yourself and upload it to Vyxor. Your broker credentials are never shared, entered, or stored anywhere in Vyxor.
Is my trade data private and secure?
+
Your trade data is stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules that only allow your account to read your own data. No other user or admin can see your trades. We never sell data. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
How does the AI analysis work?
+
Your trade history — emotions, setups, sessions, P&L — is sent to Claude AI (by Anthropic) which identifies patterns in your trading. It tells you which emotions lead to losses, which setups have the best win rate, and what specific habits are costing you money. The AI never gives buy/sell signals — it analyses your past behaviour to help you improve.
Is Vyxor a SEBI registered advisor?
+
No. Vyxor is not a SEBI registered investment advisor and does not provide buy/sell recommendations or trading signals. Vyxor is an educational platform to help traders track, analyse and improve their own trading discipline. All tools are for informational and educational purposes only.
What Indian instruments are supported?
+
NSE/BSE Index F&O: NIFTY, BANKNIFTY, FINNIFTY, SENSEX, MIDCPNIFTY, BANKEX. Stock Options: RELIANCE, TCS, INFY, HDFCBANK and 20+ more. MCX Commodities: Gold, GoldMini, Silver, CrudeoOil, NaturalGas, Copper, Zinc and more. All with correct lot sizes pre-loaded for accurate P&L calculation.
