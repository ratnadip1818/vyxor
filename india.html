Is Vyxor really free? What's the catch? ↓
Vyxor is free to start — 30 trade entries per month at no cost. No credit card needed. Vyxor was built by Ratnadip, a futures trader from Ahilyanagar who couldn't afford the ₹2,000–₹8,000/year journals. So he built his own. The position size calculator is unlimited and free. A Pro plan for unlimited trades is coming soon at ₹199/month.
How do I import my Zerodha trades? ↓
Go to console.zerodha.com → Reports → Tradebook → select your date range → Download. You'll get a CSV file. In Vyxor, go to Journal → Import tab → Zerodha section → upload the CSV. Vyxor automatically matches your buy and sell legs into complete round-trip trades and calculates P&L with correct lot sizes.
Does it work for options trading (CE/PE)? ↓
Yes. The journal has instrument type selection — Futures, Options CE, Options PE, Equity, ETF. When you select Options CE or PE, extra fields appear for strike price and expiry date. Lot sizes are applied correctly for options trades too.
Is my trade data safe and private? ↓
Yes. Your trade data is stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules that only allow your account to read your own data. No other user can see your trades. We never sell data to third parties. You can delete your account and all data anytime.
Is Vyxor a SEBI registered advisor? ↓
No. Vyxor is not a SEBI registered investment advisor and does not provide buy/sell recommendations or trading signals. Vyxor is an educational platform to help traders track, analyse and improve their own trading discipline. All tools are for informational and educational purposes only.
Does it work on mobile? ↓
Yes. Vyxor is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. Log trades from your phone during market hours. The journal is designed to be quick to fill in — you can log a trade in under 30 seconds after your exit.
