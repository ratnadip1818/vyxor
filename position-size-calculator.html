Frequently asked questions

Position sizing for Indian and global markets.

What is the NIFTY lot size?
+
NIFTY futures and options have a lot size of 25 units per lot. So 1 lot of NIFTY = 25 units. If NIFTY moves 100 points, P&L per lot = 100 x 25 = Rs 2,500. BANKNIFTY lot size is 15 units. FINNIFTY is 40 units.
How do I calculate position size for NIFTY futures?
+
Formula: Risk Amount in Rs divided by (Stop Loss in points x Lot Size). Example: Rs 10,000 risk, 50 point stop, NIFTY lot size 25. Lots = 10,000 divided by (50 x 25) = 8 lots. Vyxor calculates this instantly.
What is the MCX Gold lot size?
+
MCX Gold standard contract lot size is 100 grams. MCX Gold Mini lot size is 10 grams. Silver standard is 30 kg. Crude Oil is 100 barrels. All correct values are pre-loaded in Vyxor calculator.
What is a good risk per trade for Indian traders?
+
Most professional traders risk 0.5% to 2% of their account per trade. For a Rs 5,00,000 account, that is Rs 2,500 to Rs 10,000 per trade. Never risk more than 2% on a single trade.
