Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use Vyxor we collect:

  • Account information — email address and display name
  • Trade data — asset names, P&L, emotions, session tags, setup tags, screenshots, notes, and self-awareness fields you log
  • Usage data — which features you use and when you log in
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy, we never see your card or bank details

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the journaling, analytics, and Journal AI self-reflection features
  • To generate AI-powered insights from your trade history
  • To save and display your weekly performance reports
  • To maintain your trading streak and account preferences
  • To communicate important account and service updates

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

We never sell your personal data. We never share your trade data with third parties for any purpose. Your trading activity is completely private.

4. AI Processing

When you use the Journal AI self-reflection feature, your journal entry data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate behavioral insights. This data is used only to generate your analysis and is not stored or used for training by Anthropic under their API terms.

5. Payments

All payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. When you upgrade to Pro, Lemon Squeezy handles your payment information securely. Vyxor only receives confirmation that a payment was completed — never your card or bank details.

6. Cookies

Vyxor uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database storage
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis generation
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and subscription management
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless API functions

8. Your Rights

  • Export your trade data anytime using the CSV export feature
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Update your account information at any time
  • Your logged trades are never deleted regardless of your subscription status

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Vyxor is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform.
