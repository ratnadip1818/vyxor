Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

TL;DR — Plain English
  • We only collect your email if you voluntarily sign up for updates
  • We never sell your data to anyone, ever
  • All calculator inputs stay in your browser — we never see them
  • We may use Google Analytics to understand traffic (anonymous)
  • You can ask us to delete your email anytime

1. Who we are

Vyxor ("we", "us", "our") operates the website vyxor.in — a free trading risk management platform. We are based in India. Contact: hello@vyxor.in

2. What data we collect

We collect only two types of data:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily enter it in our notification form to be notified about new tools. This is optional.
  • Anonymous analytics — if we use Google Analytics, it collects anonymous browsing data (pages visited, country, device type). No personal identifiers.

We do NOT collect: your name, trading data, calculator inputs, financial information, or payment details. All calculator inputs are processed in your browser only and never sent to our servers.

3. How we use your data

  • Email: to notify you when new Vyxor tools launch. We do not send marketing emails or spam.
  • Analytics: to understand how many people use Vyxor and which features are most used, so we can improve the platform.

4. Data storage

Email addresses submitted through our notification form are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the Vyxor team. We do not use third-party email marketing services.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers, brokers, or any other party.

6. Cookies

Vyxor uses minimal local storage to remember your preference (e.g. if you have already subscribed to notifications). We do not use advertising cookies. If Google Analytics is enabled, it uses its standard anonymous cookies.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your email from our list at any time
  • Opt out of any future communications

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@vyxor.in

8. Children's privacy

Vyxor is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Vyxor after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests: hello@vyxor.in
