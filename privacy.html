Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

TL;DR — Plain English
  • We collect only what we need: your email (if you sign up) and your trade data (so we can show it back to you)
  • We never sell your data to anyone, ever
  • Your trade data is private — only you can see it
  • Calculator inputs stay in your browser — we never see them
  • You can delete your account and all data anytime by emailing us

1. Who we are

Vyxor ("we", "us", "our") is a free trading discipline platform operated as a brand by an independent founder based in Ahilyanagar, Maharashtra, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data when you use vyxor.in.

Contact for privacy questions: hello@vyxor.in

2. What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary for the platform to work:

  • Email address — collected only when you voluntarily submit it through our notification form, or when you create an account.
  • Account information — if you sign up for the AI Trade Journal, we collect your name and email through Firebase Authentication (a Google service). If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture as provided by Google.
  • Trade journal data — any trades you log in the AI Trade Journal (entry, exit, P&L, notes, emotions, etc.) are stored in our database (Firebase Firestore) and linked to your account.

We do NOT collect: payment information, trading account credentials, broker logins, calculator inputs (these stay in your browser only), or any data you don't explicitly enter.

3. How we use your data

  • Email — to notify you about new Vyxor features and updates. We do not send marketing emails or promotional spam.
  • Account info — to authenticate you and let you access your trade journal across devices.
  • Trade data — to display your trades back to you and generate analytics and AI analysis on your own data. Only you can see your trade data.

4. Where data is stored

We use the following third-party services to operate Vyxor:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — handles user sign-in and account credentials. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Firebase Firestore (Google) — stores your trade journal data. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only your authenticated account can access your own data.
  • Vercel — hosts the website. Vercel does not have access to your trade data.
  • Anthropic API (Claude) — when you request AI analysis, anonymized trade summaries are sent to Anthropic to generate the response. No personally identifiable information is sent.
  • Google Apps Script (Sheets) — email addresses submitted through notification forms are stored in a private Google Sheet.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not share your data with advertisers, brokers, or signal providers.

Data is only shared with the service providers listed in Section 4, strictly for the purpose of operating the platform.

6. Cookies and local storage

Vyxor uses minimal browser storage:

  • Authentication cookies set by Firebase, required to keep you signed in.
  • localStorage to remember small preferences (e.g. that you've subscribed to notifications, so we don't show the banner again).

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party advertising trackers.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of all email communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@vyxor.in. We will respond within 7 business days.

8. Data security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, Firebase's authentication system, Firestore security rules that prevent users from accessing each other's data, and a backend proxy that protects our AI API key from client-side exposure.

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to notifying you promptly in the event of a data breach affecting your information.

9. Children's privacy

Vyxor is not intended for users under the age of 18. Trading is a high-risk activity not suitable for minors. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

Vyxor is operated from India but accessible globally. By using the platform, you consent to your data being processed in India and by the service providers listed in Section 4, who may store data in their own regional data centers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered users. Continued use of Vyxor after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:
