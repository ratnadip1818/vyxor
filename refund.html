Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with Vyxor. If you are not happy for any reason, we will make it right.

Free Trial

Vyxor offers a 15-day free trial with no credit card required. You will never be charged without your explicit consent. During the trial you can log trades, view history, access analytics, and export your data.

30-Day Money Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your Pro subscription for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your first payment and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

  • Applies to first-time purchases only
  • Valid within 30 days of the initial charge
  • Refund processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refund issued to the original payment method via Paddle

Subscription Cancellation

  • Cancel your subscription at any time from your account
  • After cancellation you retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds for unused days after the 30-day guarantee period
  • Your trade data is never deleted when you cancel

Pricing

Vyxor Pro is $5/month. Payments are processed by Paddle. All prices are in USD.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at hello@vyxor.in with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund — optional, helps us improve

We will process your request within 2 business days.

Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Accounts suspended for violating our Terms of Service
  • Requests made after 30 days from the initial charge
  • Renewal charges where cancellation was not completed before the renewal date
